# Voice and Tone Guidelines

## Brand Persona
- **Professional:** We are experts in industrial engineering.
- **Reliable:** Our components are of the highest quality and genuine.
- **Supportive:** We help customers find the right technical solutions.
- **Modern:** We use cutting-edge technology and clean digital interfaces.

## Voice Attributes
- **Direct:** Use clear, concise language. Avoid fluff.
- **Technical but Accessible:** Use proper terminology but explain value clearly.
- **Trustworthy:** Focus on authenticity and genuine products.

## Content Rules
- Use title case for headings.
- Keep Thai translations formal yet natural.
- Ensure technical specifications are accurate.

## Examples
- **Good:** "High-precision bearings for industrial performance."
- **Bad:** "We sell stuff and it's pretty good for you maybe."
